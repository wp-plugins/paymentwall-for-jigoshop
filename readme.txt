== Installation ==
1. Upload `paymentwall-for-jigoshop` directory to the plugins directory.
2. Go to the plugins setting page and activate \"Paymentwall for Jigoshop\"
3. On the left sidebar of your WordPress dashboard, navigate to \"Jigoshop --> Settings\". Click on the **Payment Gateways** tab. **Paymentwall** should already be available as an option.
4. Find Paymentwall section, and check **Enable Paymentwall Solution**.
5. Enter your project and secret keys, widget code, and fill up the other options.
    * The **Project key** and Secret key are located in your \"Paymentwall Merchant Area --> My Projects\".
    * The **Widget Code** is located at the Widgets page.
6. Click the **Save Changes** button

View our full installation guide: https://www.paymentwall.com/us/documentation/Jigoshop-WordPress/1168
